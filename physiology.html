Physiology is the study of how living systems function. Scientists who study physiology are called physiologists. Physiologists attempt to describe biological phenomena in physical and chemical terms. Physiologists come to the field with extremely diverse backgrounds and apply their specialized skills and knowledge to understanding the function of living systems.
